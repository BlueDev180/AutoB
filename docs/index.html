How to Play
1) Buy units (buying removes the offer).
2) 3-of-a-kind merges → ★ up.
3) Traits give bonuses at breakpoints.
4) Augments every 3 rounds (and from elites).
5) Items drop and can be equipped (1 slot per unit).
6) Permadeath: units that die in battle are removed.
