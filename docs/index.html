ROGUE WAR
Buy units, merge 3-of-a-kind, fight, and survive. Units can die permanently.
How to Play
1) Buy units from the shop (purchases remove that offer).
2) 3 of the same unit + star level merges into a higher star.
3) Start Fight. Ranged units fire projectiles; melee units swing.
4) Permadeath: units that die in battle are removed from your roster.
5) Win for more gold. Lose HP on defeat. Survive as long as you can.
